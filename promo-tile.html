How to capture this at exactly 440×280

  1. Press Cmd + Shift + 4, then tap Space.
  2. Click the blue tile above. macOS saves the screenshot to your Desktop.
  3. On a Retina display the capture will be 880×560 — open it in Preview, Tools → Adjust Size, set width to 440, leave "Scale proportionally" on, save as PNG.

Alternative: pixel-perfect via DevTools

  1. Open this page in Chrome, press F12 to open DevTools.
  2. Right-click the tile in the Elements panel → Capture node screenshot.
  3. You get exact 440×280 PNG with no resizing needed.
